How to Vote & Submit
1
Start Xbox Game Bar recording
Press Windows + G then hit record, or press Windows + Alt + R to start immediately. Do this before opening the browser. Video saves to Videos\Captures as an MP4.
2
Open an incognito window
Open Chrome and press Ctrl + Shift + N. Go to the Newsweek voting page. Each fresh incognito window is a new session.
3
Select your product and click Submit
Click Vote Now, select the correct product from the ballot, and hit Submit. Open a new incognito window (Ctrl + Shift + N) and repeat. Stay on screen the whole time.
4
Stop recording
Press Windows + Alt + R to stop. Find your MP4 in Videos\Captures.
5
Come back here and upload
Click Submit Votes above, enter your name, drop in the video, and click verify. Claude does the rest — no need to report anything else.
